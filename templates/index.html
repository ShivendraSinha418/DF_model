Our Model

We developed a deepfake detection model using a CNN-based architecture. Input images are preprocessed with Local Binary Pattern (LBP) and resized to (299, 299, 3). The model includes convolutional layers, Global Average Pooling, Batch Normalization, and Dropout, and is trained using binary crossentropy to classify images as real or fake with a confidence score.

Results

Our model has shown an accuracy of 91% and generalizes well on the Real world data.
